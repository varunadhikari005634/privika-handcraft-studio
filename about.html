PRIVIKA HANDCRAFT STUDIO

Privika Handcraft Studio, founded by Priyanka Thapliyal, is a premium destination for luxury Indian handicrafts, antique decor, handcrafted heritage products and timeless artistic creations inspired especially by the cultural richness of India.

Our journey is driven by exploration, craftsmanship and a passion for preserving India’s artistic heritage. We travel across different regions of India to discover rare handcrafted treasures, traditional artistry, luxury decor pieces and authentic artisan creations.

Every product at Privika Handcraft Studio reflects elegance, authenticity, heritage and timeless luxury. From antique collectibles to handcrafted decor, each creation carries cultural significance, artistic excellence and traditional craftsmanship.

Our luxury handicraft collections are ideal for elegant interiors, premium gifting, luxury home decor, artistic styling and sophisticated living spaces.
