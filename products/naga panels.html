Naga Panels

Naga Panels are handcrafted wooden decorative panels inspired by the rich tribal heritage of the Naga communities of Northeast India. Featuring intricate carvings, cultural motifs, and rustic elegance, these panels add character, warmth, and a distinctive artistic touch to homes, hotels, cafes, and luxury interiors. Each piece reflects traditional craftsmanship and timeless heritage.
