Naga Tables

Nagaland Tables which are often called Naga tables are made of single wood. Traditionally these tables were used as beds by the tribals of the northeast. Each piece is hand-crafted from solid natural wood, shaped and finished using traditional techniques passed down through generations of skilled artisans. It's a timeless expression of wood, craft, and legacy. The visible grain patterns, natural knots, and subtle imperfections are not flaws, but signatures of authenticity, making every table truly one of a kind. Over time, the wood develops deeper character, telling a story of use, touch, and time. These tables effortlessly bridge heritage and contemporary living—perfect as a coffee table, floor seating table, or statement piece in minimalist and luxury spaces alike.
